Specifications of the Apple Watch Series 4


Processor:
Apple dual-core 64-bit S4
Display:
1.78 inch, LTPS OLED Retina 448x368 pixels resolution
Operating system:
WatchOS 5
Storage:
16 GB internal storage
Wireless technology:
NFC, 802.11 b/g/n WiFi, Bluetooth 5.0, and GPS/Glonass/Galileo. LTE is available on select models too.
Sensors:
32 g-force accelerometer, barometer, gyro, ECG electrode, optical heart rate, ambient light
Other features:
IP68 and 5 ATM dust and water resistant, integrated microphone and speaker
Battery:
Up to 18 hours of advertised battery life
Dimensions:
40 and 44mm models. Large one measures 44 x 38 x 10.7 mm and weighs 48 grams.
